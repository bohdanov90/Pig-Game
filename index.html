Pig Game

Player 1

Total Score: 45
Current Score: 12

Player 2

Total Score: 58
Current Score: 0

RULES

  1. This game is for 2 players. Player 1 always starts the game.

  2. Every time you roll dices the sum on your dices is added to your current score unless:

    • you rolled 1 at least on one of the dices;

    • you rolled the highest double (6+6);

    If one of the conditions is met you lose your current score and turn.

  3. After each roll you have a chance to add your current score to your total score by pressing "Hold Points" button. If you do so you save your scores but lose turn.

  4. If you decided not to "Hold" your points you roll dices again and have chance to multiply your current points. This will continue happening unless you decide to "Hold Points" or one of events from paragraph 2 happens.

  5. Game finishes when one of the players' Total Score reaches Final Score. Final Score equals 100 points by default but you can change it.

  6. That's it. Have fun!
